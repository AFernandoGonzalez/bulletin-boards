Student Affairs Introduction

As you progress along your academic journey at LaGuardia Community College, we want you to know that you have a wide variety of services, programs and staff available to assist you. Student Affairs is committed to that assistance. On this page are links to available resources dedicated to helping you accomplish tasks common to LaGuardia students.

We encourage you to view and explore all the information available within this page and all linked content. If you should have any additional questions, please feel free to contact us.

Student Affairs Mission

The LaGuardia Community College Division of Student Affairs prepares students to become informed, engaged and ethical decision-makers that embrace the responsibilities of global citizenship. This is achieved by a holistic student-centered approach, student life activities, and an array of support and developmental services that foster academic program completion. We are committed to assisting students in becoming successful graduates with a sense of purpose, autonomy, integrity, identity and broadmindedness.
